About ACE

The Association of Civil Engineering (ACE) at IIT Palakkad is a vibrant community dedicated to fostering excellence in civil engineering education and research. Our association brings together students, faculty, and industry professionals to create a dynamic platform for learning, innovation, and professional growth.

Through workshops, seminars, and hands-on projects, we aim to bridge the gap between academic knowledge and practical applications, preparing our members for successful careers in civil engineering.

Our Vision & Mission

To be a leader in civil engineering education and research, preparing graduates to address global challenges.

Our mission is to provide an exceptional learning environment that fosters critical thinking, creativity, and a strong foundation in civil engineering principles.
