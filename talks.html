Expert Talk: "Urban Governance and Smart Cities"
Shri Sivaguru Prabhakaran IAS
Commissioner, Coimbatore Municipal Corporation
Abstract:
Shri Sivaguru Prabhakaran IAS delivered an insightful talk on the challenges and opportunities in urban governance, focusing on the development of smart cities. He discussed the importance of sustainable planning, citizen engagement, and the integration of technology in municipal administration.
About the Speaker:
Shri Sivaguru Prabhakaran is the Commissioner of the Coimbatore Municipal Corporation and has played a pivotal role in implementing innovative urban solutions in Tamil Nadu. His leadership has contributed to the transformation of Coimbatore into a model smart city.
Expert Talk: "Water Resources and Irrigation Management"
Shri Priyesh R
Chief Engineer (IDRB), Irrigation Department of Kerala
Abstract:
Shri Priyesh R shared his expertise on water resource management and the critical role of irrigation in sustainable agriculture. The talk covered modern irrigation techniques, water conservation strategies, and the challenges faced by Kerala in managing its water resources.
About the Speaker:
As Chief Engineer at the Irrigation Department of Kerala, Shri Priyesh R has overseen several key projects aimed at improving water distribution and efficiency. His work has been instrumental in advancing the state's irrigation infrastructure.
Expert Talk: "But, What About Cybersecurity in AECFM?"
Prof. Bharadwaj R. K. Mantha
Professor, IIT Bombay
Abstract:
Prof. Bharadwaj R. K. Mantha discussed the emerging importance of cybersecurity in Architecture, Engineering, Construction, and Facility Management (AECFM). The session highlighted vulnerabilities, best practices, and the need for awareness among civil engineers regarding digital threats.
About the Speaker:
Prof. Bharadwaj R. K. Mantha is a faculty member at IIT Bombay with research interests in construction management, digital technologies in civil engineering, and cybersecurity in AECFM. He is recognized for his contributions to both academia and industry.
