About Us

The Association of Civil Engineering (ACE) at IIT Palakkad is dedicated to fostering a vibrant and engaging community for students interested in civil engineering. Our mission is to bridge the gap between theoretical knowledge and practical application, providing students with opportunities to learn, innovate, and connect with professionals in the field.

Through a variety of activities, including workshops, seminars, industrial trips, and expert talks, ACE aims to enhance the academic and professional development of its members. We provide a platform for students to explore different facets of civil engineering, gain hands-on experience, and network with peers and faculty.

ACE is committed to promoting a culture of learning, collaboration, and innovation, preparing students to become future leaders and contributors to the civil engineering sector.
